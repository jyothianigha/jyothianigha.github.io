About

Dr. Jyothi R.L. is an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at College of Engineering Karunagappally, where she has taught for nineteen years. Her doctoral and postdoctoral work centres on document image analysis — most distinctively, the recognition and restoration of ancient Grantha script preserved on palm-leaf manuscripts.

Her published work spans binarization, thinning algorithms, wavelet-based recognition, Gabor-based multilayer architectures and convolutional neural networks, several of which were developed specifically to recover and digitize historical palm-leaf documents that are otherwise fading from record.

Beyond the classroom, she has coordinated multiple faculty development and training programmes in image processing, Python, and open-source tools, and has led industrial visits and expert sessions connecting students with practitioners in cybersecurity and IoT.
